Call for Paper

The Editors of the Philippine Journal of Materials Science and Nanotechnology (ISSN 2362-8073) are inviting manuscript submissions for the upcoming issue (Volume 11, July 2025) of the journal.

PJMSN is a peer-reviewed, open-access journal that publishes original research in all aspects of materials science, nanotechnology, and significant research across scientific disciplines. It is abstracted under the Philippine E-Journals Index.

For submission instructions, please refer to the journal website at www.pjmsn.org.

Deadline for submission is on 31 May, 2025.

Gil Nonato Santos, Editor in Chief
Jose Isagani Janairo, Associate Editor
Michael Angelo Promentilla, Associate Editor
