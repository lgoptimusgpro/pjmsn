about

Welcome to the Philippine Journal of Materials Science and Nanotechnology, a distinguished platform designed to provide researchers with an avenue to showcase their significant contributions in the realms of Materials Science and Nanotechnology. Our journal is committed to fostering the dissemination of valuable research findings, whether they stem from undergraduate or graduate research endeavors.

By offering this platform, we aim to cultivate a vibrant community of scholars, where the exchange of ideas and the sharing of groundbreaking discoveries can thrive. The research showcased in our journal not only adds to the collective knowledge base but also serves as a catalyst for progress in various scientific and technological domains.
